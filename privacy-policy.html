Privacy Policy for DUMA

Effective date: May 7, 2026

DUMA ("the app", "we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data may be collected, how it is used, and what choices you have when using DUMA.

Privacy contact: lvisoiu300@gmail.com

1. What DUMA is

DUMA is a mobile word game. The app does not require account creation and does not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or other directly identifying information in order to play.

2. Data stored locally on your device

DUMA stores certain gameplay data locally on your device in order to function properly, including:

  • campaign progress
  • unlocked levels
  • words found in each level
  • preferred language
  • number of free hints used on the current day
  • letter or bonus hint balance
  • remove-ads ownership status, if purchased

This data is stored locally using standard operating system storage mechanisms. We do not operate our own account system, cloud sync server, or gameplay backend, and we do not upload this gameplay data to our own servers.

3. Advertising, consent, and Google AdMob

DUMA may display advertising through Google AdMob, including banner ads, interstitial ads, and rewarded ads. The app uses Google's User Messaging Platform flow for ad consent and privacy choices where those options are available or required.

When ads are shown, or when the app checks whether ads may be shown, Google and its partners may automatically collect certain technical or usage-related data depending on your device configuration, Google account settings, consent choices, and the advertising services available on your device.

This data may include, for example:

  • IP address or approximate location derived from it
  • device identifiers or advertising identifiers
  • device, operating system, app, and network information
  • diagnostic and performance information
  • interactions with ads or with the app

This data may be used for serving ads, fraud prevention, invalid traffic detection, ad performance measurement, diagnostics, security, and service improvement.

More information:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Mobile Ads SDK data disclosure

4. Firebase Analytics, Crashlytics, and Remote Config

DUMA uses Firebase services for aggregated analytics, app stability, and remote configuration:

  • Firebase Analytics helps us understand how the app is used. The app may send events such as level started, level completed, hint used, shop opened, purchase attempt, purchase success, and language changed. These events may include technical or gameplay parameters such as level number, app language, word count, remaining hints, or purchased product ID. We do not set our own User ID and we do not send names, email addresses, or phone numbers to Firebase.
  • Firebase Crashlytics helps us identify crashes and non-fatal errors. Reports may include device information, app version, operating system, stack traces, technical logs, and context keys such as the current level, language, letter count, ads state, or remove-ads ownership state.
  • Firebase Remote Config lets us change certain configuration values without publishing an app update, such as interstitial ad frequency or hint rewards. Remote Config may use technical data such as country, language, time zone, platform version, OS version, Firebase app ID, package name, and SDK version.

Firebase and Google Analytics may automatically collect certain technical data, such as app-instance ID, advertising ID, masked IP address or coarse location derived from it, app lifecycle events, in-app purchase information, Firebase identifiers, device information, and session information. This data is used for analytics, stability, diagnostics, fraud prevention, service maintenance, and app configuration.

More information:

  • Firebase privacy and security
  • Firebase Android data disclosure
  • Google Analytics for Firebase data disclosure

5. Google Play Billing

DUMA may use Google Play Billing for optional purchases, such as removing ads or buying letter packs. Payment processing is handled by Google Play. DUMA receives information needed to deliver the purchase, such as product ID, purchase status, and technical verification data. We do not directly collect your payment card details and we do not process payments ourselves.

6. Data we do not directly collect

DUMA does not directly collect your full name, email address, phone number, postal address, precise location, contacts, photos, microphone input, camera data, passwords, or login credentials.

7. Purpose of processing

We use local data only to operate the game, preserve progress, manage language, hints, and purchases. Data processed by Google, Firebase, AdMob, or Google Play is handled under their own policies for analytics, advertising, purchase delivery, security, fraud prevention, diagnostics, and service improvement.

8. Data retention

Data stored locally by DUMA remains on your device until you clear the app storage, uninstall the app, or reinstall the app without restoring local data, if applicable. We do not retain your gameplay progress on our own servers because DUMA does not currently use its own account system or cloud sync backend.

Data processed by Google, Firebase, AdMob, or Google Play is retained according to their own policies and retention periods.

9. Security

We aim to limit data use to what is necessary for the app to function. Data transmitted to Google/Firebase services is protected in transit according to those services' mechanisms. However, no method of storage or transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

10. Your choices and rights

Depending on your country or region, you may have rights regarding access, deletion, objection, or restriction of certain data processing activities. Most data directly controlled by you can be removed locally by clearing app data or uninstalling the app.

You can manage ad privacy choices in the app where those choices are available, and through your Google or device settings. For data processed by Google, Firebase, AdMob, or Google Play, please review their own privacy policies and user controls.

11. Children

DUMA is not specifically directed to children under 13.

12. International data transfers

Data processed by third-party services such as Google, Firebase, AdMob, or Google Play may be processed on servers located outside your country of residence, including in jurisdictions where those providers operate.

13. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect legal, technical, or product changes. Any updated version will include a new effective date.

14. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or about data processing in DUMA, contact us at lvisoiu300@gmail.com.
