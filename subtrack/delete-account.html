Delete your account & data

Last updated: 7 June 2026

Subtrack respects your right to remove your data at any time. This page explains exactly what gets deleted, how to request it, and how long it takes.

1. What you can delete yourself, right now

Open Subtrack → Settings → Sign out. This immediately wipes the following from your device:

  • Every subscription you added
  • Your display name
  • Your selected plan
  • The last cloud-sync timestamp

If you only used the app as a guest (no Google sign-in), nothing else exists — this is a full erasure.

2. If you signed in with Google, request a cloud deletion

When you signed in with Google, a copy of your subscriptions was saved to our private Firestore database under users/{your-uid}/subscriptions. The "Sign out" step above does not remove that cloud copy.

Email us to delete the cloud copy and any feedback you submitted. We'll erase everything tied to your account within 30 days.

Send the request to lvisoiu300@gmail.com with the subject "Subtrack delete account" from the same Google account you used to sign in to the app.

Request deletion by email

3. What gets erased

  • Firestore — your users/{uid} document and the entire subscriptions sub-collection.
  • Firebase Authentication — your Google-linked auth record is removed, so signing in again creates a fresh account.
  • Feedback collection — feedback messages tied to your user id are deleted as well. (Anonymous feedback with no user id stays in the analytics pool, since it can no longer be linked back to you.)
  • Local data on your devices — the next sign-in on a fresh install will not download anything; if you still have the app installed and signed in, use "Sign out" inside the app to wipe the device copy.

4. What we keep (and why)

  • Aggregated analytics events (no user id attached) — used to track usage trends.
  • Crash reports — anonymised stack traces with no personal identifiers.
  • Anything you may have purchased through Google Play Billing — receipts are managed by Google, not by us. You can review and refund purchases through your Google Play account.

5. Response time

We reply within 72 hours to confirm we received your request, and complete the deletion within 30 days as required under GDPR Article 17.

6. Need help?

For any privacy-related question: lvisoiu300@gmail.com.
